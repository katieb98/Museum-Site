Welcome

Welcome to Adore Boutique, where fun and funky mix with classy and comfy to create a wardrobe full of treasures! With clothes sourced from all over the world, Adore Boutique, located in the historic Downtown San Luis Obispo, is a one-stop shop for feeling fabulous, funky and strong. Come visit and get personal shopping assistance from our in-house fashionistas, and see what you can find!

Meet Adore

Getting its start amongst the glamour of Venice Beach, Adore Boutique has landed in San Luis Obispo to cater to all fashionistas in SLO County! For more than 30 years, owner Jennifer Dawes has been combing the world of fashion for the best finds, stocking only the truly unique in our Higuera Street location. We adore you, and you'll adore yourself when draped in our wonderful finds in the finest fabrics and brands. We have something for any event you can think of, so drop in for a visit and let our personal shoppers help you find something fabulous! Meet Jen and take a peek into her fabulous life on Facebook!
